# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in ÖppenBokföring a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive:** respectful language, constructive feedback, empathy, community focus.

**Unacceptable:** harassment, trolling, insults, publishing others' private information, other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report violations to the repository maintainer ([@vomba](https://github.com/vomba)). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
